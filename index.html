Who are the Avengers?

The Avengers are a group formed by General Nicholas Fury to protect America against superhuman threats. The people part of the avengers have special powers and abilities to defend te Earth against threats that may be too imminent and massive to handle. The Avengers consists of Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and more.
